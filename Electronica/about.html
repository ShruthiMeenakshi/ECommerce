Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a team of electronics engineers, ElectroMart began as a small shop in Bangalore serving local makers and repair technicians.

Frustrated by the poor quality components and unreliable suppliers in the market, we set out to create a different kind of electronics company - one that prioritizes quality, reliability, and customer service above all else.

Today, we serve customers across India with same-day shipping on thousands of genuine components, backed by our industry-leading 1-year warranty on all products.
